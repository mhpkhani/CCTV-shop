Industry Solutions

Tailored Security Plans

Every environment has unique security needs. We design solutions that address the specific risks of each sector.

Home Security

⚠️ Problem: Theft, break-ins, package theft and monitoring of elderly/children.
✅ Solution: 4-camera HD indoor/outdoor system with night vision, mobile alerts and cloud backup.

Office / Corporate

⚠️ Problem: Employee monitoring, data theft, unauthorized access and after-hours intrusions.
✅ Solution: IP camera network with access control, face recognition and centralized NVR management.

Retail / Shops

⚠️ Problem: Shoplifting, billing fraud, employee theft and disputes with customers.
✅ Solution: Wide-angle + POS-integration cameras with AI analytics to detect suspicious behaviour.

Schools / Colleges

⚠️ Problem: Student safety, unauthorized entry, bullying and campus incident accountability.
✅ Solution: Campus-wide coverage with motion zones, loudspeaker integration and 24/7 recording.

Industrial / Warehouse

⚠️ Problem: Inventory theft, equipment damage, safety compliance and perimeter breaches.
✅ Solution: Explosion-proof and weatherproof cameras with PTZ, IR floodlights and alarm integration.
